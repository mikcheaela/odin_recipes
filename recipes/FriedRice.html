Fried Rice

An immaculate bowl of spam fried rice. Extremely simple and delicious. This is an easy meal to whip up after a hard day at work or school, or to satisfy a craving you've had all day. Regardless of the motive, creation of this dish will guarantee a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 6 ounces Spam, diced
  • ½ cup frozen corn
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • ½ cup frozen diced carrots
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon fish sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sriracha, for serving
